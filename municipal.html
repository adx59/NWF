Municipal Level




New Way Forward Strategies provides a variety of services for Municipal Level campaigns. At this level, we focus upon maximizing name recognition and fostering ties with community groups




Field Operations


We develop the infrastructure required to conduct field work like canvassing, phone banking, and text banking using data from the voter database. Graphic design services, email outreach infrastructure, and social media work will be provided if necessary. If clients need mailers to be packaged, lawn signs to be distributed, emails that need to be sent, a relevant social media presence, or any other campaign literature, our team can handle it.





Volunteer Developement


We specialize in volunteer development. Our contacts with school counselors from across the nation serve as a robust base for recruiting youth volunteers to your specifications. We handle training and turf assignments for field staff, as well as developing field outreach leadership structure and office placement strategy.





Fundraising


To maximize efficacy, we provide number vetting and donor research services. Additionally, we provide logistic support for fundraising events along with follow-through services for potential donors.





Message Consulting


Analyzing and gauging the effectiveness of campaign messaging are integral to a campaign’s success. We use election data, demographic information, and key issues of the client’s race to not only determine what campaign messages resonate best with voters, but also how different campaign themes affect different groups of voters. Our methods seek to best effectivize voter communication.





Strategic Consulting


At the multi-county level, factions and coalitions represent highly relevant points of influence and contact for voters. We will utilize our current body of research to create a full outreach strategic outline for clients.


Strategic outlines include but are not exclusive to:

-an analysis of relevant demographic groups and counties, cities, and neighborhoods;

-in depth research into community leaders and officials who are influential with key communities along with leaders and communities alienated by other candidates;

-an in depth analysis into the client’s opposition to determine inconsistencies in their messaging and points of weakness in their campaign.


We will also provide endorsement outreach to facilitate introductions with local leaders and provide in-depth background information.
