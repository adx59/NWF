Careers




In the case New Way Forward is involved in a highly competitive race, all officers can expect to be required to engage in voter outreach to the best of their ability.




Research


Research officers are expected to be proficient in opposition, demographic, and donor research. Additionally, officers can expect to be involved in script creation and other technical research oriented assignments.





Operations


Operations officers will primarily engage in running voter outreach operations through phone banking and canvassing. Additionally, officers can expect to assist other departments if necessary.





Data Analytics


Data analytics officers will be involved in developing campaign websites, managing and updating the company website, data entry, and working with algorithms to efficize voter and social media outreach.





Communications


Communications officers handle campaign literature such as brochures, flyers, and social media posts. Additionally, officers can expect to be involved in translations (if possible), press releases, graphic design, and other jobs the campaign requires.
